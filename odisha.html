RATA YATRA :DALMA

Dalma, a traditional dish from Odisha, holds a special place during festive occasions like Ratatouille Yatra. This delightful preparation combines lentils, assorted vegetables, and aromatic spices. To make it, first, thoroughly rinse 1 cup of toor dal and pressure cook it with 2 cups of mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, and salt until soft. In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of ghee or oil and temper mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chilies, curry leaves, ginger, and green chilies. Add the cooked dal and vegetable mixture, simmer, and then stir in grated coconut. Adjust the consistency and let it simmer a bit more to meld the flavors. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving. This wholesome dish pairs perfectly with steamed rice or roti, offering a delicious taste of Odisha's culinary heritage during the Ratatouille Yatra¬†festivities.
