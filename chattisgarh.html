DASARA FESTIVAL: CHANNA SAMOSA

During Dasara in Chhattisgarh, Channa Samosa is a beloved snack consisting of a crispy pastry filled with spicy cooked chickpeas. It is deep-fried until golden brown and served hot with chutneys. A staple during festive gatherings, it represents the joy and flavor of the season.
