DIWALI:MILK PEDA

Diwali, celebrated fervently in Uttar Pradesh the beloved festival food is milk peda,Diwali signifies the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil. Homes shimmer with diyas, candles, and vibrant decorations. Rituals include worshiping Goddess Lakshmi for prosperity and exchanging sweets and gifts. Peda, a beloved Uttar Pradesh sweet, takes center stage during Diwali and other festivities. Made from khoa, sugar, and aromatic spices like cardamom, it boasts a creamy texture and rich flavor. Often adorned with nuts, peda is a symbol of joy and togetherness.Prepared in households, peda is shared with loved ones, enhancing the festive spirit. Its melt-in-your-mouth goodness embodies the cultural richness of Uttar Pradesh's culinary traditions during Diwali, spreading sweetness and happiness among all.
