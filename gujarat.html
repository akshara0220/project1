DASARA FESTIVAL: DHOKLA

Dhokla is one of Gujarat‚Äôs most iconic snacks, often enjoyed during festivals like Dasara. This steamed, soft, and spongy cake made of fermented rice and chickpea flour batter is both light and healthy. During Dasara celebrations, it is often served alongside chutneys and garnished with mustard seeds, green chilies, and fresh coriander.

The festive atmosphere of Navratri and Dasara in Gujarat brings people together to enjoy Garba and Dandiya dances, while food stalls serve flavorful dishes like Dhokla, Khandvi, and Fafda-Jalebi. Dhokla remains a favorite because of its delicious taste and health benefits.
