GANGUAR FESTIVAL:DAL BATTI CHURMA DISH

Dal batti churma during the Gangaur festival in Rajasthan is one can savor the delightful culinary tradition. This iconic dish comprises three main components, each contributing to its rich flavor and texture. Firstly, there's the dal, a savory lentil preparation infused with aromatic spices, offering a hearty and comforting taste. Then comes the batti, a dense, round bread made from wheat flour, traditionally baked in a charcoal or wood-fired oven, imparting a smoky essence. Finally, there's the churma, a sweet and crumbly mixture of coarsely ground wheat flour cooked with ghee and sugar, adding a delightful sweetness to the ensemble.
