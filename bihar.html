CHHATH PUJA: THEKUA

Thekua holds a special place during Chhath Puja in Bihar. This sweet snack made of wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee is offered to the Sun God and later shared among devotees. It's crisp, rich, and flavored with cardamom and fennel. Women prepare it with devotion as part of the Prasad during this major festival celebrated with sunrise and sunset rituals along riverbanks.
