HERATH FESTIVAL: ROGAN JOSH

The Herath Festival, also known as the Kashmiri Shivratri, is a revered Hindu celebration held in February to honor Lord Shiva. Kashmiri Pandits mark it with prayers, night vigils, and traditional dishes like Rogan Josh.

Rogan Josh is a beloved Kashmiri delicacy made with tender lamb simmered in a fragrant curry of Kashmiri chili, fennel, yogurt, and other native spices. This iconic dish is often slow-cooked in copper pots and served during special occasions like Herath.
