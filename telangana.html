BATHUKAMMA:SAKINALU

During dusshera bathukamma is celebrated in telangana and beloved snack during this festival is Sakinalu, are crispy treats made from rice flour, sesame seeds, cumin seeds, ajwain, and salt. The dough is formed by mixing these ingredients with water to a medium consistency and left to rest. Portions of the dough are then shaped into thin cylindrical forms or intricate patterns before being deep-fried until golden brown and crispy. The frying process imparts a delightful crunchiness and enhances the flavors of sesame and cumin. Once fried, Sakinalu are drained of excess oil and allowed to cool before serving. These savory snacks are enjoyed during tea-time or as a festive treat, adding to the joyous atmosphere of Bathukamma celebrations. Their irresistible texture and subtle spiced flavor make them a favorite among both children and adults alike, further enhancing the festive spirit of the occasion.
