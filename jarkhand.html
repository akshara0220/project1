SARHUL FESTIVAL: THEKUVA DISH

Thekuva, also known as Tekua, is a deep-fried sweet made from jaggery and rice flour. Traditionally prepared during the Sarhul Festival in Jharkhand, it's enjoyed as a symbol of prosperity and community. Thekuva is crisp on the outside and soft inside with a hint of cardamom.
